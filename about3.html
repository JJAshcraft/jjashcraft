JJ is an engaging communicator with an intriguing history...


After the death of his brother in a tragic car accident at age three, JJ Ashcraft struggled with a fear of death that plagued him through childhood. After many failed attempts during his teenage years to break his fears, he found a tool that changed everything -

Sensory Deprivation Tanks.

By combining sensory deprivation and visualization techniques, JJ learned how to overcome his fears and push the boundaries of what he thought was possible in his own life. In the process, JJ partnered with two others and opened Gravity Spa, a floatation center in Dayton, OH. He figured out how to help others overcome their fears, find their big ideas, and cut away from obstacles holding them back. After selling his portion of the business, he decided to put his new skills to the ultimate test...what would prove to the world that these methods are legitimate?

In a struggle between overcoming fear and finding passion, JJ takes a literal leap from 13,000 feet to find out that his deepest fears brought about a love for flying (and a new business) he never would have expected. Where will your fears take you?


"Our minds are incredibly powerful. When left unchecked, they can turn a simple thought into an epic action movie, where we can either play the hero or the victim in an imaginary battle of will to live out our desires."

JJ Ashcraft
